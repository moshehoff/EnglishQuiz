# English Grammar Workbook – Class 7

## דף עבודה 4

1. She ________________________________ (live) in Tel Aviv for ten years.

2. You ________________________________ (must / have to) finish your homework before dinner.

3. If I ________________________________ (study) harder, I would have passed the exam.

4. I saw ________________________________ (a / an / the) interesting movie last night.

5. We ________________________________ (visit) London two years ago.

6. I enjoy ________________________________ (read / to read) books in the evening.

7. The meeting starts ________________________________ (in / on / at) 3 o'clock.

8. This is ________________________________ (tall / taller / the tallest) building in the city.

9. Look! It ________________________________ (rain) outside right now.

10. The book ________________________________ (which / who / where) I bought yesterday is very interesting.

11. By next month, I ________________________________ (finish) my project.

12. There are ________________________________ (much / many / a lot of) students in the classroom.

13. Please ________________________________ (turn on / turn off) the light when you leave.

14. If I ________________________________ (be) rich, I would travel around the world.

15. They ________________________________ (study) when the phone rang.

16. She ________________________________ (can / must / should) speak three languages fluently.

17. He ________________________________ (go) to the gym every Monday.

18. My car is ________________________________ (expensive / more expensive / the most expensive) than yours.

19. They ________________________________ (be) friends since they were children.

20. If it ________________________________ (rain) tomorrow, we will stay home.
