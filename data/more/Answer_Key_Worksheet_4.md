**Answer Key - Worksheet 4**

1. I **have never tried** sushi before.  
**הסבר:** Present Perfect עם never.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I have never visited Paris.

2. While she **was talking** on the phone, the baby **started** crying.  
**הסבר:** Past Continuous + Past Simple.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** While I was driving, I saw an accident.

3. You will pass the test if you **study** hard.  
**הסבר:** First Conditional.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** If you hurry, you will catch the bus.

4. This house **was built** in 1995.  
**הסבר:** Past Simple Passive.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** The bridge was opened last year.

5. We **might** go to the cinema tonight.  
**הסבר:** Modal "might" – אפשרות.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** It might rain tomorrow.

6. The book **that** I read yesterday was amazing.  
**הסבר:** Relative Clause עם that.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** The movie that we watched was boring.

7. Look at the sky! It **is going** to rain soon.  
**הסבר:** Going to for prediction.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She is going to win the game.

8. She is the **tallest** girl in the class.  
**הסבר:** Superlative.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** This is the most expensive phone.

9. I am interested in **learning** new languages.  
**הסבר:** Gerund אחרי "interested in".  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She is good at drawing.

10. They arrived **at** the airport **on** time.  
**הסבר:** Prepositions (at the airport + on time).  
**דוגמה נוספת:** We arrived at school on time.

11. He is **an** honest person.  
**הסבר:** Article "an" לפני תנועה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She is an intelligent student.

12. I have very **little** free time.  
**הסבר:** Little עם uncountable (time).  
**דוגמה נוספת:** There is little water left.

13. She did the test very **carefully**.  
**הסבר:** Adverb.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** He spoke politely.

14. My parents **have lived** in Tel Aviv for 25 years.  
**הסבר:** Present Perfect עם for.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** We have studied English for many years.

15. I called my friend **to** invite her to my birthday.  
**הסבר:** Infinitive of purpose.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I went to the kitchen to make coffee.

16. There **is** some water in the bottle.  
**הסבר:** There is + uncountable.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** There is some milk in the fridge.

17. Why **didn’t** he **answer** the phone?  
**הסבר:** Past Simple בשאלה שלילית.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** Why didn’t you come yesterday?

18. I hurt **myself** while playing football.  
**הסבר:** Reflexive pronoun.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She burned herself while cooking.

19. She has **just arrived** home.  
**הסבר:** Present Perfect עם just.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I have just finished my homework.

20. He sometimes **plays** basketball after school.  
**הסבר:** Present Simple עם sometimes.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She often reads books in the evening.
