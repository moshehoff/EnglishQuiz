**Answer Key - Worksheet 5**

1. How long **have** you **known** your best friend?  
**הסבר:** Present Perfect בשאלה עם How long.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** How long have you lived here?

2. I was sleeping when you **called** me.  
**הסבר:** Past Simple (פעולה קצרה).  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She arrived when I was eating.

3. If we **don’t hurry**, we will miss the bus.  
**הסבר:** First Conditional שלילי.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** If you don’t study, you won’t pass.

4. The food **is served** by the waiters.  
**הסבר:** Present Simple Passive.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** Dinner is served at 8 o’clock.

5. Students **should** respect their teachers.  
**הסבר:** Modal "should" – המלצה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** You should help your parents.

6. This is the restaurant **where** we ate last week.  
**הסבר:** Relative Clause עם where.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** This is the place where I was born.

7. I **am not going** to the party tomorrow.  
**הסבר:** Present Continuous שלילי לעתיד.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** We are not coming next week.

8. Mathematics is **more difficult** than history for me.  
**הסבר:** Comparative.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** English is easier than math.

9. He stopped **smoking** last year.  
**הסבר:** Gerund אחרי stop.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She started learning Spanish.

10. She goes to school **by** bus.  
**הסבר:** Preposition "by" לאמצעי תחבורה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I go to work by car.

11. Mount Everest is **the** highest mountain in the world.  
**הסבר:** Superlative עם the.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She is the smartest student.

12. There isn’t **much** traffic today.  
**הסבר:** Much עם uncountable (traffic).  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I don’t have much time.

13. He looked at me **angrily**.  
**הסבר:** Adverb.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She smiled happily.

14. She **works** in a bank now.  
**הסבר:** Present Simple.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** He lives in Tel Aviv.

15. I study hard **to** get good grades.  
**הסבר:** Infinitive of purpose.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She exercises to stay healthy.

16. **Are there** any chairs in the room?  
**הסבר:** There is/are בשאלה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** Are there any questions?

17. What **is** your brother **doing** these days?  
**הסבר:** Present Continuous.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** What are you reading now?

18. We **enjoyed** ourselves on vacation.  
**הסבר:** Reflexive pronoun.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** They enjoyed themselves at the party.

19. I have **seen** this film three times.  
**הסבר:** Present Perfect.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She has visited Jerusalem twice.

20. She usually **doesn’t eat** meat.  
**הסבר:** Present Simple שלילי.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** He doesn’t like chocolate.
