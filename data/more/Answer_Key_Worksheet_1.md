**Answer Key - Worksheet 1**

1. have never been  
   **הסבר:** Present Perfect + never  
   **דוגמה:** She has never visited Paris.

2. was studying / was making  
   **הסבר:** Past Continuous - שתי פעולות בו זמנית  
   **דוגמה:** While I was eating, my phone was ringing.

3. won  
   **הסבר:** Second Conditional  
   **דוגמה:** If I won a million dollars, I would help my family.

4. was made  
   **הסבר:** Past Simple Passive  
   **דוגמה:** The book was written in 2020.

5. should  
   **הסבר:** Modal for advice  
   **דוגמה:** You should exercise more.

6. who  
   **הסבר:** Relative pronoun for people  
   **דוגמה:** The man who lives upstairs is friendly.

7. will snow  
   **הסבר:** will for prediction  
   **דוגמה:** I think it will be hot tomorrow.

8. best  
   **הסבר:** Superlative  
   **דוגמה:** This is the best restaurant in the city.

9. dancing  
   **הסבר:** Gerund after "good at"  
   **דוגמה:** He is excellent at swimming.

10. at / at  
    **הסבר:** Prepositions of place and time  
    **דוגמה:** We arrived at the station at 9.

11. an  
    **הסבר:** Article before vowel sound  
    **דוגמה:** There is an orange on the plate.

12. few  
    **הסבר:** Few with countable nouns  
    **דוגמה:** I have few problems.

13. well  
    **הסבר:** Adverb  
    **דוגמה:** She dances beautifully.

14. is running  
    **הסבר:** Present Continuous  
    **דוגמה:** The children are playing outside.

15. to  
    **הסבר:** Infinitive of purpose  
    **דוגמה:** I went home to rest.

16. were  
    **הסבר:** There were (past)  
    **דוגמה:** There were many students.

17. wakes up  
    **הסבר:** Present Simple - habit  
    **דוגמה:** He wakes up at 6 every day.

18. too  
    **הסבר:** Too = יותר מדי  
    **דוגמה:** This shirt is too small.

19. since  
    **הסבר:** Since + point in time  
    **דוגמה:** I have lived here since 2018.

20. doesn’t like  
    **הסבר:** Present Simple negative  
    **דוגמה:** She doesn’t like chocolate.
