**Answer Key - Worksheet 2**

1. She **has already finished** her homework.  
**הסבר:** Present Perfect עם already – פעולה שהסתיימה לאחרונה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I have already eaten lunch.

2. When I called her, she **was watching** a movie.  
**הסבר:** Past Continuous – פעולה ממושכת בעבר.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** While I was eating, she was talking on the phone.

3. If it rains, we **will stay** at home.  
**הסבר:** First Conditional – מצב אפשרי בעתיד.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** If you study hard, you will pass the test.

4. English **is spoken** in many countries.  
**הסבר:** Present Simple Passive.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** Hebrew is spoken in Israel.

5. He **must** finish the project by tomorrow.  
**הסבר:** Modal "must" – חובה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** You must wear a seatbelt.

6. Tel Aviv is a city **where** many tourists visit.  
**הסבר:** Relative Clause עם where (למקום).  
**דוגמה נוספת:** This is the hotel where we stayed.

7. We **are going** to the beach next weekend.  
**הסבר:** Present Continuous for future plans.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I am meeting my friends tomorrow.

8. My phone is **more expensive** than yours.  
**הסבר:** Comparative form.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** This car is faster than that one.

9. I hate **waiting** in long lines.  
**הסבר:** Gerund אחרי hate.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She loves swimming in the sea.

10. She was born **in** May **in** 2009.  
**הסבר:** Prepositions of time (in + month + year).  
**דוגמה נוספת:** He was born in December in 2010.

11. This is **the** most beautiful song I know.  
**הסבר:** Superlative עם the.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** This is the best day of my life.

12. How **much** money do you need?  
**הסבר:** Much – עם uncountable nouns (money).  
**דוגמה נוספת:** How much water do you drink?

13. He runs very **quickly**.  
**הסבר:** Adverb (quickly).  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She sings beautifully.

14. I usually **eat** breakfast at 7:30.  
**הסבר:** Present Simple להרגל.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** We always go to school by bus.

15. She saved money **so that** she could buy a new laptop.  
**הסבר:** So that – לביטוי מטרה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I study hard so that I can get good grades.

16. **Is there** any milk in the fridge?  
**הסבר:** There is / There are בשאלה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** Is there any bread left?

17. What time **do** you usually **go** to bed?  
**הסבר:** Present Simple בשאלה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** Where do you live?

18. I enjoyed **myself** at the party.  
**הסבר:** Reflexive pronoun.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** We enjoyed ourselves on vacation.

19. She **told** me that she was tired.  
**הסבר:** Told + someone.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** He told us the truth.

20. I have known him **for** three years.  
**הסבר:** For + תקופת זמן.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She has lived here for ten years.
