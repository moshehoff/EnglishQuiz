**Answer Key - Worksheet 3**

1. They **have not seen** this movie yet.  
**הסבר:** Present Perfect שלילי עם yet.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I haven’t finished yet.

2. It was raining when we **left** the house.  
**הסבר:** Past Simple (פעולה קצרה) + Past Continuous.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She arrived when I was cooking.

3. If I were you, I **would not buy** that phone.  
**הסבר:** Second Conditional.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** If she were here, she would help us.

4. The letter **was sent** yesterday.  
**הסבר:** Past Simple Passive.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** The book was published last year.

5. You **don’t have to** come if you don’t want to.  
**הסבר:** Don’t have to = אין חובה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** You don’t have to finish it today.

6. The teacher **who** helped me is very kind.  
**הסבר:** Relative Clause עם who.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** The man who called is my uncle.

7. I **am going to visit** my grandparents this weekend.  
**הסבר:** Going to for plans.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She is going to study tonight.

8. This is the **worst** day of my life.  
**הסבר:** Superlative.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** This is the funniest story.

9. He promised **to help** me.  
**הסבר:** Infinitive אחרי promise.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She decided to stay home.

10. We live **on** the fifth floor.  
**הסבר:** Preposition "on" עם קומות.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** They live on the third floor.

11. She is **an** engineer.  
**הסבר:** Article "an" לפני תנועה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** He is an actor.

12. There are too **many** cars in the street.  
**הסבר:** Many עם עצמים ספירים.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** There are too many people.

13. The music is **loud**.  
**הסבר:** Adjective אחרי "is".  
**דוגמה נוספת:** The movie was interesting.

14. Right now I **am writing** a grammar worksheet.  
**הסבר:** Present Continuous.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** They are playing now.

15. He went to Australia **to** learn English.  
**הסבר:** Infinitive of purpose.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** I came here to see you.

16. There **are** many books on the shelf.  
**הסבר:** There are + plural.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** There are three chairs.

17. How often **do** you **exercise**?  
**הסבר:** Present Simple בשאלה.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** How often do you go swimming?

18. This exercise is not difficult **enough** for me.  
**הסבר:** Not + adjective + enough.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** She is not old enough.

19. I have **lived** here since I was a child.  
**הסבר:** Present Perfect עם since.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** We have known each other since 2018.

20. She always **forgets** her keys.  
**הסבר:** Present Simple עם always.  
**דוגמה נוספת:** He never arrives late.
